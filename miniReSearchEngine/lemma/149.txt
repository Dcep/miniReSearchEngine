Photo : Alamy By Nicole Blackmore 3:42PM BST 27 Jun 2014 Follow Comments Most of u know that to cut our energy bill we should turn thing like television and computer monitor off at the wall rather than leave them in standby mode , which consume power even when theyre not be use . But small gadget and appliance quietly run in the background could be push your energy bill up much fast than you realise . The bad culprit in most home be in fact a wireless router , which cost 21.92 a year on average while on standby , accord to Ecotricity . The green electricity company have compile a list of the bad household offender and the result may come as a surprise . A laser printer for example cost 18.26 a year to leave on standby , while a desktop PC cost 3.65 and a plasma television cost 4.87 .
