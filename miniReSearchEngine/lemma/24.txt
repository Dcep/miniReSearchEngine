If the Massachusetts Department of Public Health ( DPH ) , work with the MassDEP , determine that there be a water-borne disease outbreak , MassDEP and MassDPH will advise the PWS and consumer of any other precaution that be necessary . Who can be affect ? Anyone who ingests contaminate water may become ill . Infants , young child , the elderly , and people with severely compromise immune system be more at risk of illness . Why must I boil my water ? A boil order have be issue to your water system because either recent testing have show the presence of organism that could cause illness ( e.g . fecal or E. coli bacteria ) , or technical/physical problem in the water system have significantly increase the possibility of bacterial contamination . How can I make my water safe ? Boiling the water be the best way to ensure that it be free of illness-causing organism . Bring water to a rolling boil for a minimum of one minute . When it cool , refrigerate the water in clean container . ( A pinch of salt per quart may improve the rather flat taste of boil water ) . If you do not want to boil your water , you can disinfect it by add 1/8 teaspoon of bleach ( common household bleach contain 5.25 percent sodium hypochlorite ) per gallon of water . Do not use bleach contain perfume , dye , or other additive . Is it necessary to boil all water in the home during an advisory or order ? During boil water advisory or boil water order , you should boil all water use for drinking , prepare food , beverage , ice cube , wash fruit and vegetable , or brush teeth . Severely immuno-compromised individual should always boil their tap water for the purpose note above . Infant formulas should be prepare use boil tap water , at all time . In the event that boil be not practical , the PWS may recommend an alternative supply know to be safe ( e.g. , bottle water ) or may direct you to disinfect the water use household bleach . It be not necessary to boil tap water use for other household purpose , such a showering , laundry , or bath . Adults , teen , and old child can wash , bathe , or shower ; however , they should avoid swallow the water . Toddlers and infant should be sponge-bathed . How should tap water be boil properly ? Water should be place in a heat-resistant container or in an electric kettle without an automatic shut-off and bring to a rolling boil for 1 minute to kill all disease-causing organism . Water can also be boil in a microwave oven use a microwave-safe container , but it be advisable to include a glass rod or wooden or plastic stir stick in the container to prevent the formation of superheated water ( water heat above it boiling point , without the formation of steam ) . The water should then be cool and pour into a clean container or refrigerate until you be ready to use it . At elevation over 6,500 foot ( 2,000 meter ) water boil at a slightly low temperature and should therefore be boil for at least two minute to kill all disease-causing organism . I have a water treatment device ; do I still need to boil my drinking water ? If the device be design to improve the taste and odor or chemical quality of the water , such a activate carbon filter , it be still necessary to boil the water . Check with the manufacturer if you be not certain . Can I use bottle water ? Buying bottle water may be a feasible alternative to boiling water . Bottled water operation be routinely inspect , and sample be periodically analyze to ensure they meet health standard . What can I do with my tap water ? During a boil order , can my family take shower or bath use tap water ? The risk of bath in tap water be uncertain and so should be avoid particularly by people with open wound or who be immunocompromised . For those people who choose to shower or bathe in the tap water , minimize the time spent in the water and be sure to keep your eye and mouth close . Babies and young child should not bathe or shower in tap water because they often swallow some water accidentally . During a boil order , can I wash dish use tap water ? You may use a dishwasher if it have a sanitizing cycle . Once you have be notify that the boil order have be lift , these device should be clean and sanitize accord to the operator 's manual for the device . Can I give my pet tap water ? Although pet be not normally affect by the same disease a human , caution suggest give pet boil or bottle water . What be the symptom of water-borne illness ? Disease symptom may include diarrhea , cramp , nausea and possible jaundice and associate headache and fatigue . Symptoms may appear a early a a few hour to several day after infection and may last more than two week . These symptom , however , be not just associate with disease-causing organism in drink water ; they may also be cause by a number of other factor . If you be ill with these symptom , contact your health care provider . What if I drink the water already ? There be nothing you can do about the exposure you have already receive . If you become ill , contact your health care provider . Follow the above recommendation about use your water until you be tell the water be safe again . How long will the boil order remain in effect ? Each boil order situation be different , make it impossible to predict how long the boil order will remain in effect . It will not be lift until testing show that the water meet public health standard . Boil water advisory or boil water order be lift by the MassDEP when the water be consider safe and no longer pose a threat to public health . What should I do when the boil water advisory or order have be lift ? The MassDEP and PWS will provide specific instruction . Consumers should flush water pipe within the home . When flush it be important to carefully follow the instruction provide . Some type of water treatment device may need to be disinfect and flush to remove any contaminate water before be use . Depending on the type of water treatment device , the device may need to be replace . Check with the manufacturer for detail . A note about E. coli bacteria : E. coli be a sub-group of the fecal coliform bacteria group . There be many strain of E. coli , most of which be harmless , but some strain can cause illness . E. coli outbreaks receive much medium coverage . Most outbreaks have be relate to food contamination ( not water ) cause by a specific strain of E. coli know a E. coli O157 : H7 . When a drinking water sample be report a `` E. coli positive '' , it do not mean that this specific strain be present and in fact , it be probably not present . However , it do indicate recent fecal contamination . Boiling or treat contaminate drink water with a disinfectant destroys all form of E. coli , include O157 : H7 .
