Share Compact refrigerator be the right size to use space efficiently . They primarily store and chill beverage and snack in game room , garage , and bedroom . The drawback to their small size be that a mini fridge sometimes use more energy than a full-size refrigerator , accord to a Consumer Reports review . The actual cost to run a mini fridge depends on several factor , include the size of the refrigerator and the number of hour in use . Consumers can learn to calculate the average annual cost to run a mini fridge before purchase . Reasons for High Energy Use On average , a standard model mini fridge us roughly 230 kilowatt-hours per year , a much a many Energy Star rat full-sized refrigerator . The amount of energy a mini fridge consume be due in large part to poor insulation and lack of cool feature . Poor Insulation Due to their small size , mini fridge have great surface area per cubic foot than full-size refrigerator and less room for insulation . This lead to the generation of more heat , and that increase the interior temperature . The end result be that a compact refrigerator require more energy to keep the interior cool . No Cooling Features Full-size refrigerator use external condenser and fan to dissipate heat . In contrast , mini refrigerator use their external wall to dissipate heat , and this leaf them more sensitive to room temperature . Compact refrigerator in warm room use more energy than those use in cooler location . Calculating Energy Costs for a Mini Fridge With the number of variable that factor into appliance energy consumption , it be difficult to predict how much energy a mini fridge may use . However , there be way to estimate energy use and cost base on the wattage rating of appliance . Additionally , many appliance include energy rating tag that offer rough yearly estimate for consumer who do not want to do the math themselves . Energy Guide Labels The Federal Trade Commission require Energy Guide label on appliance such a refrigerator , washer , television , water heater , and dishwasher . These bright yellow tag provide estimate for annual yearly operating cost base on national average for usage and energy cost . Energy Guide label show consumer the range of operating cost for similarly equip model . Appliances that meet specific criterion for energy efficiency receive an Energy Star rating on their label . Calculated figure for popular model include average base on testing in controlled condition . Energy Use Calculation With a few simple calculation , consumer can estimate the operating cost of various mini fridge , even if there be no Energy Guide label attach . Each appliance rate at a specific number of watt-hour . For instance , many mini fridge rate at roughly 100 watt-hour . To find the cost of operation per hour , the consumer divide the watt-hour of the refrigerator by 1,000 and then multiplies that number by the cost per kilowatt-hour of electricity obtain from the local utility company . To calculate annual cost estimate , the consumer multiplies the cost per hour by the number of hour per week ( 168 hour if constantly run ) and multiplies that answer by 52 , the number of week in a year . Conclusion Although mini fridge consume a disproportionate amount of energy , most consumer do not notice the slight increase in their electricity bill because most compact refrigerator cost less than $ 5 per month to operate . For most people , the convenience of have cold beverage readily available when playing game or watch sport in garage or basement be often enough to justify the extra cost . Consumers can manage energy consumption by choosing model with Energy Star rating and keep the room with the fridge a cool a possible .
