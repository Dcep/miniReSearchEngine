Step 1 : Do your research Before purchase a solar PV system , it important to make sure the investment will be worth your while . That mean identify the best type and size of solar PV system for your property , a well a look into the government incentive available in your area . Find out more about the cost and saving of go solar . Step 2 : Contact your electricity retailer Not all electricity company offer solar-friendly policy . Youll want to contact your electricity retailer to find out : how solar will affect your current rate what sort of feed-in tariff they offer . A feed-in tariff be the amount an electricity retailer pay you for excess power generate by your solar panel . Our Guide to Installing Solar PV for Households includes a list of question to ask your electricity company . Step 3 : Find a solar PV retailer and plan your system Its important to shop around when buy solar . Before you select a system , talk to different solar PV retailer about your option and obtain several quote . You also want to make sure that the company you buy your system from be ethical , reliable and will be there if something go wrong . The best way to do this be by choosing a Clean Energy Council Approved Solar Retailer . Approved Solar Retailers follow industry best practice , use fully accredit installers and offer a minimum five-year guarantee on the entire system . Read more about find a solar retailer or view our list of Approved Solar Retailers . Step 4 : Sign a contract Once you have choose your system and accept a quote , your solar PV retailer , installer or designer will provide you with a contract . There be a few thing about the contract you should check before sign . Our Guide to Installing Solar PV for Households have all the detail . Step 5 : Install your solar PV system Your solar PV retailer or installer will install your system and provide you with all the necessary documentation . Remember to be eligible for government rebate , your system must be instal by a Clean Energy Council accredit installer . An Approved Solar Retailer will always use accredit installers . If youre not buy from an approve retailer , ask to see your installers accreditation card or ensure they be list on our find an installer page . Step 6 : Connect your solar PV system to the grid Your solar PV retailer will usually arrange for your solar system to be connect to the electricity network on your behalf . Grid connection process differ from state to state . Please refer to our small-scale connection page if you would like more information . Step 7 : Use and maintain your solar PV system Congratulations ! Your solar PV system be instal , and it time to start saving money on your power bill . To keep your system work efficiently and safely , youll need to make sure it be properly maintain . Find out more about what to do once your system be instal . Want more information ?
