Charger Voltage and Power Consumption

Answer

Warning: DO NOT plug into an outlet with a voltage other than specified on the bottom of the charger or sanitizer. Voltage converters DO NOT guarantee voltage compatibility.

Flexcare: 100-240 V, 50-60 Hz 0.4 watts @ 100 V, 1.4 watts @ 240 V
HealthyWhite: 100-240 V, 50-60 Hz 0.4 watts @ 100 V, 1.4 watts @ 240 V

Essence: 110-120 V, 50-60 Hz 0.9 watts @ 110 V, 1.1 watts @ 120 V
Elite: 110-120 V, 50-60 Hz 0.9 watts @ 110 V, 1.1 watts @ 120 V
Intelliclean: 110-120 V, 50-60 Hz 1.5 watts
