Purchasing an energy-efficient A/C unit will help you realize significant savings year after year. We recommend you look over our A/C Buying Guide for great tips and suggestions. Purchasing a new A/C system is a big investment and we are here to help. In order to qualify for a valuable A/C Buying Rebate, you must: Purchase and install a complete high-efficiency A/C system. This includes both indoor and outdoor A/C units. Purchase a new A/C system that has a minimum SEER (Seasonal Energy Efficiency Rating) of 15. Purchase your A/C system through a Participating Independent Contractor (PIC) .
